FERRET

Scientific Name: Mustela putorius furo.

Read more

A playful and curious small mammal commonly kept as a pet. Ferrets are energetic, enjoy exploring, and interact well with humans. They sleep for long periods but are very active during playtime.

BUDGERIGAR

Scientific Name: Melopsittacus undulatus.

Read more

A small and colorful Australian parrot commonly known as a budgie. Budgerigars are friendly, intelligent, and capable of learning sounds or simple words. They are popular pets due to their playful and social personality.

BORDER COLLIE

Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris.

Read more

A highly intelligent and energetic dog breed known for its herding abilities. Border Collies are loyal, quick learners, and require plenty of physical exercise and mental stimulation. They are considered one of the smartest dog breeds.

PONY

Scientific Name: Equus caballus.

Read more

A small and gentle horse breed, typically used for riding and driving. Ponies are known for their sturdy build, friendly nature, and adaptability to various environments.

MEERKAT

Scientific Name: Suricata suricatta.

Read more

A small social mammal native to southern Africa. Meerkats live in groups called mobs or clans and are known for standing upright to watch for predators. They are curious, cooperative, and highly social animals.

MINI PIG

Scientific Name: Sus scrofa.

Read more

A small domestic pig breed kept as a companion animal. Mini pigs are intelligent, social, and can be trained similarly to dogs. They require proper care, enrichment, and a balanced diet.
